What CHOIVE is

The product you are using and what it is designed to do.

CHOIVE is an AI selection diagnostic platform. It analyses how likely AI systems — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude — are to recommend your business when a potential customer asks for one. It scores your current position, identifies who AI recommends instead of you, and tells you exactly what to fix.

What CHOIVE is not
  • A guarantee that AI will recommend you
  • A ranking service or SEO tool
  • A legal or professional advisory service
  • A prediction of any specific commercial outcome
  • An endorsement of your business by CHOIVE
What CHOIVE is
  • An honest diagnosis of your current AI selection position
  • A live analysis of real signals at the time of the diagnostic
  • An automated AI assessment — not a human opinion
  • A starting point for informed action, not a final verdict
  • A product that tells the truth, even when the truth is uncomfortable

AI recommendation behaviour is determined by the AI platforms themselves. CHOIVE analyses the signals that influence that behaviour — it does not control it. No diagnostic tool can guarantee how any AI platform will respond to any specific query on any given day.

Accuracy and limitations

What your result means.
And what it doesn't.

CHOIVE generates diagnostic results using live data — search signals, website inspection, citation checking, and AI simulation — at the exact moment you run the diagnostic. Those signals change constantly. A result from Monday may differ from one run on Friday. That is not a defect. It is the product accurately reflecting a live and shifting environment.

Your result is a snapshot, not a permanent judgement. AI recommendation behaviour across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude shifts as those platforms update their models, change their training data, and adjust their response behaviour. CHOIVE measures your position at a point in time. It cannot predict how those platforms will behave tomorrow.

On score variation between runs. Running the same diagnostic twice in 24 hours may produce slightly different scores. This is expected and correct — it means the signals CHOIVE is measuring are genuinely live. A score that never changes regardless of what you do would be the lie. CHOIVE does not lie.

CHOIVE is not professional advice. Nothing in your diagnostic result constitutes legal, financial, marketing, or professional advisory guidance. It is an AI-generated analysis of observable signals. Act on it with the same judgement you would apply to any business intelligence tool.
