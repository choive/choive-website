Yes. The Report is built from your real diagnostic data — your score, your competitor, your pillar breakdown, your specific gaps. Without a diagnostic, there is no data to personalise the report from. The free diagnostic takes 45 seconds and requires no signup.
How quickly do I receive the Report?
Usually within a few minutes. Once payment is confirmed, the CHOIVE engine automatically builds the PDF from your diagnostic data and emails it to you. No manual request is required.
What format is the Report?
An HTML file optimised for saving as PDF. Open it in any browser and click "Save as PDF" — or use the print button at the top right. It is designed to render exactly like a premium consulting document at any paper size.
Is every Report different?
Each report uses a consistent ten-section structure, but its business name, recorded evidence, pillar scores, competitors, dates, and prioritized actions come from that diagnostic run.
Can I share the Report with my team or agency?
Yes — that is exactly what it is designed for. The competitor signal table, the 30-day plan, and the ready-to-use assets are all built to be actioned by a team. Most business owners share it with their developer the same day they receive it.
What if I want a Report based on updated signals?
Run a new free diagnostic. Unlock the Analysis. The Report button will appear at the bottom of your new paid result — built from your updated data. Each Report is tied to the diagnostic that generated it.
