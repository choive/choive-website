CHOIVE vs Profound: which one do you actually need?

Profound provides enterprise AI visibility monitoring. CHOIVE runs a focused, point-in-time recommendation diagnostic. They serve different needs. Here is the practical difference.

The short version: choose Profound when your team needs continuous enterprise monitoring. Choose CHOIVE when you need a one-time answer showing which businesses four AI platforms recommend, the evidence behind your score, and the next improvements to prioritize.

Two different products for two different moments

Profound is a monitoring platform. It tracks, continuously and at enterprise depth, how a brand appears across AI engines — publicly reported to have raised $155M at a $1B valuation, with Fortune 500 clients and custom, sales-led pricing. It is the category’s reference product for large teams who already run an AI visibility program.

CHOIVE asks Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini separately whether they recommend the business or another company. It inspects public website and search evidence, scores Clarity, Trust, Difference, and Ease, and provides prioritized improvements and ready-to-use assets in the paid products.

CHOIVE does not promise that an official API response will match every consumer-app session. Apps can use different models, search context, settings, conversation history, or personalization. CHOIVE reports the provider response recorded during that diagnostic run.

Choose honestly

Choose Profound if…

  • You are an enterprise brand with a six-figure AI-visibility budget
  • You have a dedicated SEO / content team to act on deep monitoring data
  • You already know where you stand and need continuous tracking across many markets

Choose CHOIVE if…

  • You don’t yet know whether AI recommends you — or your competitor
  • You want the answer in 30 seconds, free, with no sales call
  • You need prioritized improvements rather than continuous monitoring
  • You are the size of business enterprise platforms are not built for

Can you use both?

Yes — and in the right order. Run the CHOIVE diagnostic first: it tells you where you stand and closes the foundational gaps. If you then grow into a continuous, multi-market AI visibility program with a team behind it, an enterprise monitoring platform is the natural next investment. Diagnose first. Monitor when there is something worth monitoring.

How CHOIVE makes the comparison

CHOIVE separates three things that are often mixed together: what each AI provider recommended during the run, which company is the closest verified purchasing substitute, and how strong the subject business's own public evidence is. The CHOIVE score is calculated from the subject business evidence, not from whether an AI provider happened to recommend it. The complete process and limitations are published in the CHOIVE methodology.
