CHOIVE vs Profound: which one do you actually need?

Profound is the enterprise leader in AI visibility monitoring. CHOIVE is the AI selection diagnostic. They solve different stages of the same problem — and choosing the wrong stage wastes either your budget or your window. Here is the honest breakdown.

The short version: if you are an enterprise brand with a dedicated team and a continuous AI-visibility budget, Profound is built for you. If you are any other business — and you first need to know whether AI recommends you at all, and exactly what to fix — that is what CHOIVE exists for. Most businesses need the diagnostic before they need the monitoring.

Two different products for two different moments

Profound is a monitoring platform. It tracks, continuously and at enterprise depth, how a brand appears across AI engines — publicly reported to have raised $155M at a $1B valuation, with Fortune 500 clients and custom, sales-led pricing. It is the category’s reference product for large teams who already run an AI visibility program.

CHOIVE is a diagnostic. It answers one question, instantly and for free: when a customer asks AI to recommend a business in your category — does it say yours? It runs real recommendation queries, inspects your live signals, scores you across four pillars (Clarity, Trust, Difference, Ease), and — in the full Report — prescribes the exact fixes in priority order, with ready-to-use assets.

The distinction industry reviewers keep making about monitoring platforms is that they show you the problem and leave the fixing to your team. That gap is the reason CHOIVE exists: other tools tell you what’s wrong. CHOIVE tells you what to do.
